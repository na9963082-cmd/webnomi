Frequently Asked Questions

What web development services do you offer?

I offer comprehensive web development services including frontend development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), backend development, database design and management, WordPress customization, and AI-powered solutions. I can build everything from simple landing pages to complex web applications.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple landing page can be completed in 3-5 days, while a full-featured web application may take 2-4 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you provide website maintenance and support?

Yes! I offer ongoing maintenance and support packages including updates, security patches, content changes, and technical assistance. I ensure your website stays secure, fast, and up-to-date.

Can you help with WordPress websites?

Absolutely! I specialize in WordPress development including theme customization, plugin development, site optimization, and custom functionality. Whether you need a new WordPress site or improvements to an existing one, I can help.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all websites I create are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. Mobile-first design is a standard practice to ensure your site looks perfect on all screen sizes.

What are AI tools in web development?

I leverage AI tools for various tasks including content generation, code optimization, automated testing, and smart chatbots. These tools help deliver faster development, better user experiences, and innovative features for your website.
